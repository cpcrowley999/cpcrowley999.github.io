Wave Field Visualization

In December 2020 ClabbCrow were contracted by Resolute Marine Energy to develop a Matlab® tool to facilitate visualization of wave fields incident to an array of wave energy converters. The attached video displays an example result, where irregular waves are shown propagating at an angle of 30 degrees relative to an array of 16 wave energy converters (represented by the red dots).
